Two Player Chess

Turn your tablet or phone into a two-player chessboard.

Highlights

Two Players

Two Player Chess features symmetrical piece and board designs that allow two people to use a single device, whilst sitting opposite each other as in a traditional game of chess.

Improve your game

Review your games and get tips from the inbuilt AI about best moves. The thicker the blue line, the better the move.

Track, Review & Share Games

Track, review and share your games from the history tab. You can also load and review games your friends share with you by pasting PGN notation.

Play vs in-built AI

Play anywhere and anytime against the inbuilt AI and improve your game. Multiple personalities are included with different strengths and realistic playing styles.

Flexible Settings

Enable or disable highlighting of legal and last moves, allow players to have uneven/asymmetric time limits and more.
